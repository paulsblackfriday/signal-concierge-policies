Signal Concierge Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 13, 2026

1. About Signal Concierge

Signal Concierge is a private Instagram customer-messaging assistant operated by Paul Nagle for the Instagram professional account @paul_san_driveaz_miyabi.

Signal Concierge helps people communicate with Paul about vehicle interests, general vehicle questions, test-drive requests, and appointment preferences.

2. Information We Process

When a person sends a direct message to the connected Instagram professional account, Signal Concierge may process information such as:

  • Instagram-scoped user, account, and conversation identifiers
  • Instagram username or display name when made available
  • Direct-message text
  • Message timestamps
  • Message delivery, read, and interaction status
  • Vehicle make, model, trim, feature, or body-style interests
  • Preferred test-drive or appointment dates and times
  • Trade-in interest
  • Name, telephone number, or email address voluntarily provided by the user
  • Other information voluntarily included in the conversation

Users should not send Social Security numbers, payment-card information, bank-account details, passwords, medical information, or other highly sensitive information through Instagram direct messages.

3. How We Use Information

Signal Concierge uses information only for purposes such as:

  • Receiving and responding to Instagram customer messages
  • Identifying the vehicle or type of vehicle a person is interested in
  • Answering general vehicle and appointment questions
  • Offering possible appointment or test-drive windows
  • Helping Paul coordinate personal follow-up
  • Notifying Paul when a conversation requires manual attention
  • Maintaining enough conversation context to provide a relevant response
  • Protecting the service against misuse, fraud, or unauthorized access
  • Complying with applicable legal obligations

4. Pricing, Financing, and Vehicle Availability

Signal Concierge does not make binding price commitments, financing decisions, credit decisions, guaranteed trade-in valuations, or guaranteed vehicle-availability statements.

Final pricing, financing, availability, and transaction terms must be confirmed directly with an authorized dealership representative.

5. Human Review and Manual Takeover

Messages may be reviewed and answered manually by Paul. Automated replies may be paused when Paul takes over a conversation or when a request requires personal assistance.

6. How Information May Be Shared

Signal Concierge does not sell personal information.

Information may be shared only with:

  • Paul, as the operator of Signal Concierge
  • Technology providers necessary to operate the messaging service
  • The dealership or appropriate dealership personnel when the user asks to schedule an appointment, visit the dealership, or continue a dealership transaction
  • Government authorities or other parties when disclosure is required by law or reasonably necessary to protect rights, safety, or security

7. Service Providers

Signal Concierge may rely on third-party infrastructure and technology providers for functions such as Instagram messaging, webhook delivery, secure connectivity, application hosting, artificial-intelligence processing, logging, and system maintenance.

These providers may process limited information only as necessary to provide their services and subject to their own terms and privacy policies.

8. Data Retention

Conversation information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to the inquiry, coordinate an appointment, maintain service continuity, protect the system, resolve disputes, or comply with applicable legal obligations.

Information that is no longer reasonably needed may be deleted or anonymized.

9. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect information processed by Signal Concierge. These measures may include restricted account access, protected access tokens, encrypted connections, limited administrative access, and system logging.

No internet-based service can guarantee absolute security. Users should avoid sending highly sensitive information through Instagram messages.

10. User Choices and Data Deletion

A user may request access to or deletion of eligible information processed by Signal Concierge.

To submit a request:

  1. Email YOUR_EMAIL_ADDRESS.
  2. Use the subject line: Signal Concierge Data Request.
  3. Include the Instagram username used to contact @paul_san_driveaz_miyabi.
  4. Describe whether you are requesting access, correction, or deletion.

Limited verification may be required to confirm that the requester controls the relevant Instagram account. Do not send passwords or government identification unless specifically and securely requested.

Eligible requests will be processed within a reasonable period, subject to information that must be retained for legal, fraud-prevention, security, or dispute-resolution purposes.

Complete deletion instructions are available on the User Data Deletion Instructions page.

11. Children's Privacy

Signal Concierge is intended for people who are legally able to inquire about vehicle shopping or appointments. The service is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

12. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the app, its data practices, or applicable requirements change. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when material revisions are made.
