Signal Concierge User Data Deletion Instructions

Effective date: July 13, 2026

You may request deletion of eligible information associated with an Instagram conversation processed by Signal Concierge.

How to Submit a Deletion Request

  1. Send an email to: paulsblackfriday@gmail.com
  2. Use this email subject: Signal Concierge Data Deletion Request
  3. Include the Instagram username you used to message @paul_san_driveaz_miyabi.
  4. Briefly state that you want information associated with your Signal Concierge conversation deleted.

Identity Verification

We may ask for limited information needed to confirm that you control the Instagram account associated with the request.

Do not email your Instagram password, Social Security number, payment-card information, bank information, or other highly sensitive identification.

What May Be Deleted

Eligible information may include:

  • Stored Instagram-scoped conversation identifiers
  • Stored direct-message content
  • Vehicle-interest information provided in the conversation
  • Appointment preferences
  • Contact information voluntarily provided in the conversation
  • Other stored conversation details linked to the verified requester

Information That May Be Retained

Certain information may be retained when reasonably necessary for:

  • Legal or regulatory obligations
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Enforcing agreements
  • Resolving disputes
  • Maintaining records of completed deletion requests
  • Protecting the rights and safety of users or other parties

Information that must be retained will be limited to what is reasonably necessary for those purposes.

Processing Time

After the requester has been verified, eligible stored information will be deleted or anonymized within a reasonable period.

A confirmation may be sent to the email address used to submit the request after processing is complete.

Removing the App Through Instagram

Users may also review or remove connected applications through Instagram's Apps and Websites settings. Removing the app may stop future access, but a separate deletion request may still be needed for eligible information previously stored by Signal Concierge.
